Here are some tips to help you improve

Have Good Timing

Make sure your timing is good. Approach your boss after you've done a good job on a project, or if you have just taken on new responsibilities.

Highlight Potential

Instead of just focusing on your past accomplishments, demonstrate to your boss why you are valuable to the company's future.

Confidence is Key.

Try power posing a couple minutes before your talk. Power posing is a technique pioneered by Amy Cuddy that is scientifically proven to help you feel more confident and calm.

Practice Makes Perfect!

Make sure you practice your pitch, paying attention to your delivery, body language, and tone. Try finding a friend to practice with.

Learn about the Wage Gap

The pay gap affects women from all backgrounds, but is worse for women of color.

While earnings increase for both men and women as they age, the gender pay gap does too.

Education increases earnings for all genders, but in some cases, the gender pay gap may increase at higher education levels.

On average, women working in the United States are paid 80% of what men are paid.
